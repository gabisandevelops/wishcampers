Wishcamper Development Partners

Company

We are passionate about this

Wishcamper Development Partner’s principals have over 100+ years of combined experience across 23 states; having developed or redeveloped over 16,500 units of affordable apartment housing since the early 1970’s. Today our development footprint resides in the Pacific Northwest and Northern Rocky Mountain Region, with offices in our home states of Portland, Oregon and Missoula, Montana.

We have never terminated a subsidized rent contract or removed an affordable housing use-restriction, we routinely adhere to Enterprise Green and Universal Design standards, and we are dedicated to improving the lives of our residents through comprehensive and fully-funded Resident Service Programs and Equity Plans that advance equal access to all opportunities within affordable housing.
